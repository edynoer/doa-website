Certifications

Formal Design Organization Approvals

DOA GMF AeroAsia operates under approvals granted by the Indonesian DGCA and EASA.
DGCA Approval

DGCA Design Organization Approval – DOA.21J.001 (Class-C)

The Indonesian Directorate General of Civil Aviation (DGCA) grants DOA GMF AeroAsia approval DOA.21J.001 with Class-C scope, authorizing:

  • Cabin interior changes & LOPA updates
  • Seat installation & reconfiguration
  • Cargo & cabin safety equipment assessment
  • Supporting analysis & compliance documentation
DGCA Approved Class-C DOA
EASA Approval

EASA Part 21.J – EASA.21J.565 (Minor Changes & Minor Repair Designs)

EASA approval EASA.21J.565 authorizes DOA GMF AeroAsia to perform:

  • Minor Change design & substantiation
  • Minor Repair development & justification
  • MoC definition & compliance verification
EASA Approved Minor Change Minor Repair

Process

How DOA GMF AeroAsia Works

Every change is assessed, justified, and approved within a controlled Design Assurance System.
  • 01
    Requirement Review Understanding objectives, constraints, and applicable regulations.
  • 02
    Feasibility & Classification Assess technical feasibility & classify change (major/minor).
  • 03
    Design & Engineering Prepare engineering data, drawings, and analysis under DOA procedures.
  • 04
    Compliance Mapping Define MoC, conduct safety assessments, and gather evidence.
  • 05
    Approval & Release Prepare DOA approval package for authority or operator acceptance.

Traceable & auditable at every stage
